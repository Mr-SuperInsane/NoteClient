## How to insert Image
You can insert an image into an article by specifying "<IMAGE>: {absolute path of the image}".

<IMAGE>: C:\Windows\Web\Wallpaper\Windows\img0.jpg
The block immediately following <IMAGE> is the image caption.
This line is part of the caption.

The next line after a blank line is outside the block.

## How to set paid line
You can set a paid line by specifying <PAID_LINE> on a single line.

<PAID_LINE>

When specifying a paid line and setting the post_setting argument of Note.create_article() to True, you must specify the price using the price argument.
